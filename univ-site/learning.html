My Disciple-Scholarship

Spiritually Strengthening

BYU has played a big part in my spiritual growth. Since being at BYU, I've been able to better understand and connect gospel principles with academic facts. I love how BYU strives to create an environment where the Holy Ghost can be felt and guide the professors in their teaching. This has helped me to grow my testimony and succeed in my academic endeavors. It is great to be surrounded by people that share my beliefs and understand the background that I come from, making it easier to create connections with others.

Intellectually Enlarging

Since I've been at BYU, I've felt my mind expand and grow in capability. I've had to work very hard to balance my learning, work, and other activities in a productive way. I've learned to be more efficient and thorough in my studies and projects. BYU has rigorous courses, and that has forced me to push myself. I've also become more motivated to gain skills and understanding of as many things as I can.

Character Building

Being at BYU has motivated me to work to grow myself and strive to attain Christlike attributes. Since my mission, I've understood the importance of trying to be more and more like Jesus Christ. This requires a lot of effort and prayer. BYU emphasizes the need to build our character and become like Jesus Christ, and I want to work towards that. I have and will continue to identify ways I can improve, then set goals and make plans to achieve those things. I have learned that often it is necessary to improve ourselves before we can accomplish certain thing or become who we want to be.

Lifelong Learning & Service

Serving others and growing my knowledge throughout my life are both things that I want to do. Learning in school is good, and of course it is concentrated more than at other times, but learning won't end when I graduate. I need to spend my life trying to grow my mind and acquire knowledge and skills. This is important for several reasons, but a major reason is that it will allow me to better serve those around me. For one, I can help others to learn things. I can also apply my skills and knowledge to do things that make a difference in the lives of others. For example, perhaps I will someday use my programming abilities to make something that helps someone in need.
