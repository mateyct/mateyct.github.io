"Inasmuch as ye shall keep my commandments ye shall prosper in the land"

This is a very common phrase in the Book of Mormon. Just how many places does it show up?

1 Nephi 2:20 1 Nephi 4:14 2 Nephi 1:9
2 Nephi 1:20 2 Nephi 4:4 Jarom 1:9
Mosiah 1:7 Mosiah 2:22 Mosiah 2:31
Alma 9:13

It's interesting that this principle is brought up almost verbatim so many times in the Book of Mormon. There are plenty of other verses that teach the same idea in a different way. I think the Lord wants us to remember this. It's not that He wants us to be righteous with the intent to get rich, but rather He wants us to know that if we work hard to follow Him and keep His commandments, He will take care of us. He also wants us to use that prosperity to help others.

Note: This is only up to where I have read in the Book of Mormon as of making this website, and it might not be complete.
